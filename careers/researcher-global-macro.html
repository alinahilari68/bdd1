Researcher, Global Macro

Back to Roles on offer

SIGNIFIC is looking for a Researcher to join its Global Macro practice.

In this position, you will support the analysis of cutting-edge trends at the intersection of international politics and markets. In addition to having a deep background in foreign affairs and political economy, the ideal candidate will be highly proficient in conducting research, data analysis, and trend identification.

In this position, you will report to a senior member of the Global Macro practice and support the work of the practice’s staff. You will also assist in developing the firm’s research agenda and reviewing research as it is produced. The ideal candidate will be highly motivated and disciplined, taking significant initiative and demonstrating sound judgment in managing competing priorities.

Knowledge of markets and economics is a plus, as is command of foreign languages.

Qualifications::

  • Master’s degree, preferably focused on finance, economics, business, policy, and/or international affairs (Bachelor’s degree required).
  • a deep background in foreign affairs and political economy, the ideal candidate will be highly proficient in conducting research, data analysis, and trend identification.
  • ~3-5 years of work experience (experience at a financial institution, management consulting firm, or other client service preferred).
