Director, Global Markets

Back to Roles on offer

We are seeking an experienced professional with at least ten years of financial markets experience to join our Global Markets Advisory team working with institutional investors where geopolitics, country risk and financial markets intersect. This role will be based in Sydney and will be specifically focused on building market share and growing our Asian-Pacific institutional client base by delivering first class service and solutions to our clients. Remote applications are accepted. For more information about SIGNIFIC please visit: http://sygnconsulting.com/.

Responsibilities:

  • Work with Global Markets leadership team to acquire new clients and achieve revenue growth amongst a portfolio of approximately 20 institutional accounts (Asset Managers, Hedge Funds, Pension funds, Investment Banks).
  • Develop a strong understanding of SIGNIFIC’s business and become an expert in how political risks fit into client investment and risk process.
  • Leverage SIGNIFIC’s global resources in country and sectoral research and market strategy to create a value-add coverage.
  • Maintain knowledge of market developments (on a multi-region basis), asset classes and political risks in order to engage with and provide value to sophisticated institutional investors
  • Develop deep client relationships with a primary focus on end-user clients.
  • Create and build in-depth strategic account plans and provide support to senior leadership in business and strategy planning (including new business pipeline) and major new pitches
  • Must be a self-starter with a strong team-focused mindset and problem-solving skills.
  • Work closely and effectively with analyst and strategy teams as a value-added partner.

    • Desired Skills：

  • Prior experience in global financial markets in a client facing role with experience and knowledge of multiple asset classes, investment styles and client types. (Global Macro background ideal)
  • Prior experience in an institutional sales or advisory role with proven ability to monetize a differentiated research & advisory offering across wide range of investor types preferred.
  • Experience working with analyst and strategy teams preferred.
  • Background in emerging markets, FX and fixed income markets as well other asset classes relevant.
  • Prior experience with account mapping, strategic planning and prospecting / pitching.
  • Demonstrable interest in SIGNIFIC’s core political risk assessment competency
  • Must be a self-starter with a strong team-focused mindset and problem-solving skills.
  • Willingness and capacity for travel on an ongoing basis.
  • Bachelors degree required, Masters or MBA preferred.
