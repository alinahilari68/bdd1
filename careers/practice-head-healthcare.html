Practice Head, Healthcare

Back to Roles on offer

SIGNIFIC is looking for a senior, high-caliber professional to lead its Healthcare Practice.

Companies see acute risks and rare opportunities from politics around the globe. As businesses invest and operate globally, the ability to foresee and manage political risks becomes critical. The need to understand how politics can impact strategy, risk management and operational decision-making is particularly acute for firms within the healthcare space.

As SIGNIFIC’s Healthcare Practice Head, you will be responsible for leading the firm’s research and analysis on public health as well as healthcare industry-related issues – including pharma, medical devices and medtech. You will also help build SIGNIFIC’s client leadership and expertise in that industry, forge long-lasting, trust-based client relationships and deliver tailored solutions to top healthcare companies around the world. Combining the rich knowledge base of SIGNIFIC’s research platform of country analysts with your authoritative expertise, analytical skills and assertiveness, you will work closely with top global clients to help them navigate uncertainty and develop solutions that turn political risks into unique opportunities.

Responsibilities:

  • Lead SIGNIFIC’s research and analysis on global public health issues, particularly regarding COVID-19
  • Lead SIGNIFIC’s research and analysis on healthcare industry issues at a global and key-market level, including regulatory policy/enforcement, healthcare reform, pricing, supply chains, R&D and IP issues
  • Lead and grow SIGNIFIC’s portfolio of clients in healthcare
  • Collaborate on country-level research and analysis
  • Structure and develop tailored client solutions, business insights and recommendations

    • Qualifications：

  • Post-graduate degree in life sciences and/or political science or a policy-related field
  • Experience in healthcare and public health policy
  • Experience in prospecting, leading and managing a portfolio of clients and senior client executives
  • Proven ability to independently manage projects and teams
